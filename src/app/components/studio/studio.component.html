Move from Idea to startup

We help entrepreneurs overcome confirmation bias and work towards real value propositions rather than perceived value propositions. Our way of doing this is by being intentional about human-centred design and allowing customers to define the product as much as possible before committing resources to building the product.

Services

Testing with customers

Understanding your customers is the key ingredient to success for any startup as it ensures product-market fit. This is why we advocate for the Genchi Genbutsu principle. With this, we facilitate entrepreneurs enrolled in our program in having direct conversations with their potential customers in a bid to help them understand and single out the most important needs. We have up to 44 proven experiments that entrepreneurs can use singly or in combinations to test our concepts, features or entire products.

Building the right team

A common trait of all successful startups is their strength in the composition of their founding team. This is the sole reason why we do a thorough evaluation of startup teams’ capabilities and experiences. We however understand that getting the right team is not always easy and that is why we have a pool of talent that startups can draw from when need arises.

Why entrepreneurs like to work with us

As much as there is no one-size-fits-all approach to venture building, our venture playbook is proven to help bring about scalable and sustainable business models for our portfolio ventures.

1

Concept validation

This marks the first phase of our venture-building process in which we work with founders to validate opportunities and build business models while ensuring alignment with the overall founder’s vision. We also help to raise the capital required for the next phases.

2

Product-market fitting

In this phase, we use HCD principles to come up with appropriate branding alongside testable prototypes that help define product feature sets. We also use Design Thinking skills to better the value propositions and improve the user experience for the target customers

3

Technology build

In this phase, we help entrepreneurs build the capacity required to achieve the technical requirements for the product. We do not teach coding skills but we can link up the venture with a tech-build partner.

4

Acceleration and commercialisation

We extend our involvement in a portfolio venture during the acceleration and commercialization phase to ensure there is sustainable organizational scaling. We also offer operational support through our studio’s access to skilled personnel who may be used as shared resources
