Why entrepreneurs like to work with us

As much as there is no one-size-fits-all approach to venture building, our venture playbook is proven to help bring about scalable and sustainable business models for our portfolio ventures.

1

Concept validation

This marks the first phase of our venture-building process in which we work with founders to validate opportunities and build business models while ensuring alignment with the overall founder’s vision. We also help to raise the capital required for the next phases.

2

Product-market fitting

In this phase, we use HCD principles to come up with appropriate branding alongside testable prototypes that help define product feature sets. We also use Design Thinking skills to better the value propositions and improve the user experience for the target customers

3

Technology build

In this phase, we help entrepreneurs build the capacity required to achieve the technical requirements for the product. We do not teach coding skills but we can link up the venture with a tech-build partner.

4

Acceleration and commercialisation

We extend our involvement in a portfolio venture during the acceleration and commercialization phase to ensure there is sustainable organizational scaling. We also offer operational support through our studio’s access to skilled personnel who may be used as shared resources
