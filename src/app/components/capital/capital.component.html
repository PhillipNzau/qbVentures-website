FAQS

01. What is QBcapital an what do you do?

QBCapital is an investments arm of QBVentures and helps African entreprenuers have access to funding in order to build and scale their ventures.

02. What is your investment criteria?

We invest in preseed and seed stage ventures that are proven to be commercially viable and investor ready

03. What is the geographic focus of QBCapital?

Our primary market is Africa. However, we are not limited to the African market provided the investment criteria have been met

04. What is your approach to woking with portfolio companies?

QBCapital works closely with founders to ensure they have a good financial posture. This paves way for further investments into a portfolio venture
