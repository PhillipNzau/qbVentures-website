ABOUT

We are an African hybrid venture studio supporting startups in Africa's fast-growing technology landscape.

Qbventures is a platform and a venture catalyst on a mission to promote innovation, invest in technology ideas (govtech, fintech and emerging markets) and also explore alternative business models.Through our team of experts, our main focus is on growing and transforming markets across Africa through integrated business and software solutions and coming up with new business models.

OUR TEAM

Our values guide our culture and approach to venture building

  • It is okay to fail
    We embrace calculated risk-taking and consider the learning opportunities in failure as integral to our growth and innovation.
  • Chutzpah - Dare to Do
    We adopt boldness, audacity, and confidence in pursuing ambitious goals and taking on challenges.
  • Innovative.
    We are open to new ideas and constantly looking for ways to improve and evolve. We strive to think outside the box and take risks in order to bring new and unique products and services to the market.
  • Passionate
    We are made up of individuals who are deeply committed to their work and are excited about the potential impact we can have.
  • Leadership
    We foster an environment where individuals are encouraged to take charge and make decisions that will drive the company forward. We also actively work on developing the leadership skills of our team members
  • Accountable
    We hold ourselves accountable to our key stakeholders for delivering on our promise. We also hold our members accountable for their work and have clear expectations for their performance and results. We constantly work to create a culture of transparency and trust that ensures that we are always moving in the right direction.
