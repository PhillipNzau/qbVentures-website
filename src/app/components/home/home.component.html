Loita is a startup company that seeks to revolutionize asset and inventory tracking and management services. To this end, Loita is actively developing a management system that combines real-time geolocation reporting with real-time analytics of telematics data to give asset and inventory managers situational awareness and insights that can be used to guide critical decisions. The system also features advanced behavioral analytics through computer vision to assess personnel behavior and perform automated reporting based on user preference.
